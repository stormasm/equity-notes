

### yield is a function of the NAV not the market price

The 30-day SEC yield for the Vanguard Core Bond ETF (VCRB) is a function of the net asset value (NAV). It is calculated by taking the net investment income earned by the fund's portfolio holdings (minus expenses) over the past 30 days and dividing it by the average NAV.

- Basis: The yield is based on the underlying portfolio value (NAV), not the market price, ensuring it reflects the income the bonds are generating.
